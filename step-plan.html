ADD-ONS (AVAILABLE AT PURCHASE):

  1. Double Leverage (25% Cost) - Point-of-Sale add-on allows the trader to use double the leverage (i.e., 1:20).
  2. No Stop Loss (10% Cost) - Point-of-Sale add-on that disables “Stoploss Required”; effectively traders can trade without having trades automatically close without a stoploss
  3. Hold Over Weekend (10% Cost) - Point-of-Sale add-on that disables “Flat for Weekend” requirement; this allows traders to keep positions open over the weekend.
  4. Share of Gains Increased to 90% (20% Cost) - Point-of-Sale add-on that increases a trader's potential share of gains for live accounts to 90% (up from the standard 75%) of the gains.
